Let's Make a Crypto-Millionaire

What if thousands of people gave 0.01 ETH each to one person?

Each round, a randomly selected beneficiary receives donations. Anybody can donate. All donors are eligible to be selected as the beneficiary for the next round. Our Ethereum smart contract handles the Ether transfers and enforces the rules.
